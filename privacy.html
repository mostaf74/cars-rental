Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement explains the personal data Carserving collects, how Carserving processes it, and for what purposes.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Transaction information including payment details.
  • Technical information like IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Process transactions and send related information, such as booking confirmations and invoices.
  • Send promotional messages and other information about our services.

Information Sharing

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Carserving except in the following cases:

  • With your consent.
  • For legal reasons, to comply with any applicable law, regulation, or legal process.

Data Security

We work hard to protect our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
